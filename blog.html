Re-finding my passion

August 27, 2014

I'm excited for this year in photojournalism to begin. I want to see how far I can push myself and how much my photography can improve this semester. I want to study as much work as I can, shoot as much as I can, and find my passion all over again. Through adjusting to school my freshman year, I somehow let my photography get away from me, and then sophomore year reinvigorated me, but now I am no longer satisfied and want to produce better work. I want to embrace every aspect of photojournalism and make this a really educational year for myself. Only time will tell.
